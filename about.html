Our History

Founded in 1995 by visionary educators, Greenfield Academy began as a small independent school with just 47 students in a converted farmhouse. Our founders believed in creating an educational environment that nurtured both intellectual curiosity and personal growth.

Over the past 25+ years, we've grown into a thriving institution serving over 800 students from preschool through 12th grade, while maintaining our commitment to individualized learning and community values.

In 2010, we moved to our current 25-acre campus featuring state-of-the-art facilities, and in 2020 we were recognized as a National Blue Ribbon School for academic excellence.
