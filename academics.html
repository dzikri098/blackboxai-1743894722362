Our Curriculum

Balanced approach combining core academics with enrichment

Core Subjects

English Language Arts

Comprehensive literacy program emphasizing critical reading, analytical writing, and public speaking.

Mathematics

Conceptual understanding through problem-solving, from basic numeracy to calculus.

Science

Inquiry-based learning in life, physical, and earth sciences with lab experiences.

Social Studies

Historical thinking, cultural awareness, and civic engagement.

Enrichment Programs

Visual & Performing Arts

Studio art, music, theater, and dance programs fostering creativity.

Technology & Engineering

Computer science, robotics, and design thinking curriculum.

World Languages

Spanish, French, and Mandarin programs emphasizing communication.

Physical Education

Health, wellness, and athletic skill development.

Special Academic Programs

Unique opportunities for advanced and specialized learning

STEM Scholars Program

Accelerated program in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics with research opportunities.

  • Advanced lab facilities
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Summer research internships

Arts Conservatory

Pre-professional training in visual arts, music, theater, and dance.

  • Master classes with visiting artists
  • Portfolio development
  • Annual showcase events

Global Studies

Interdisciplinary program focusing on international issues and cultural fluency.

  • Model UN participation
  • Study abroad opportunities
  • Language immersion

Entrepreneurship Institute

Hands-on business and innovation program developing real-world skills.

  • Startup incubator
  • Business plan competitions
  • Mentorship from local entrepreneurs
