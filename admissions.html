Application Requirements

What you'll need to apply

All Applicants

  • Completed online application
  • $75 application fee
  • School records from past 2 years
  • Teacher recommendations
  • Student and parent interviews

Lower School (K-5)

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Readiness assessment
  • Playgroup observation (K only)

Upper School (9-12)

  • Standardized test scores
  • Writing sample
  • Extracurricular resume
  • Additional recommendations

Financial Aid

Greenfield Academy is committed to making our education accessible to qualified students regardless of financial circumstances. We award over $2 million in financial aid annually.

Need-Based Aid

Awards range from 10% to 95% of tuition based on demonstrated financial need.

Merit Scholarships

Available for exceptional academic, artistic, and athletic achievement.

Payment Plans

Flexible monthly payment options available through our business office.
