Directions & Parking

By Car

From downtown, take Highway 101 North to exit 45B. Turn right on Education Avenue. Campus is 0.5 miles on the left.

Public Transit

Take Metro Line 4 to the Education Avenue stop. Walk 3 blocks north to campus.

Visitor Parking

Free visitor parking is available in Lot A near the main entrance. Check in at the security kiosk for a parking pass.
